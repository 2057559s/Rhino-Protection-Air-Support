Rhino Protection Air Support

Funding for an aircraft to act as air support for an independent volunteer Rhino protection unit.

Find Out More

YOU CAN MAKE A DIFFERENCE!

The situation for the Rhino population is a lot worse than is generally perceived by the public. This year alone more than 500 Rhino's have been lost due to poaching. The criminals responsible have no regard for the animals and are brutal and cruel, severely injuring the Rhinos and leaving them to die from their horrendous injuries. I have therefor volunteered my skills as a pilot to a pro-active approach with Idefix Flight Travel and a private Rhino Protection Unit in Southern Africa, to fight the crimes committed against Rhinos and raise awareness of the issue. The unit is currently working on an air-support presence and requires assistance in this establishment. All funds raised on this campaign will go towards the operational costs of the aircraft within the unit. All flying will be done on a volunteer basis by me. If you are not comfortable funding my efforts directly then you are encouraged to consider a donation to the broader Rhino protection campaign at www.stoprhinopoaching.com where you can find more information about the problem at hand.
